Amplius is about expanding your mind through the ideas of others.
A lot of people in the course of history have gone through a lot of experience, and sometimes the best (and less painful) way to learn is through the experience of others.
Quotes are a good way to share wisdom. They are small, they are concise, they share a lesson... it is like twitter for the past generations!
You can learn quotes from books, movies, friends, and now there's Amplius to collect them all!

In Amplius you can share your own experience through quotes that have helped you in some point in your life.
You can also see what has helped others when going through your same situation.
Become an Ampliuser and earn amps daily:

  • Share your quote
  • Give your quote some background by saying where did you read it / heard it
  • If some quote helped you, give the ampliuser your gratitude
